## Coffee Vending Machine Sales Analysis


### Project Overview
We are junior data analysts on the sales team at a coffee vending franchise company in Ukraine. Over the past three months, we have observed a decline in sales. The director has requested a report to determine whether the current sales decline is related to trends, consumer behavior, or product issues.
 
So main objective of this project is : 
1. Analyzing the sales data to identify patterns or anomalies.
2. Investigate factor that associated sales decline.
3. Provide possible recommendation to address the sales decline and improve future performance.
4. Compile the findings & recommendation into a report.
