## Coffee Vending Machine Sales Analysis


### Project Overview
We are junior data analysts on the sales team at a coffee vending franchise company in Ukraine. Over the past three months, we have observed a decline in transactions. The director has requested a report to determine whether the current decline is related to trends, consumer behavior, or product issues. So we have an objectives for this project : 
1. Analyzing the transactions data to identify patterns or anomalies.
2. Investigate factor that associated transactions decline.
3. Provide possible recommendation to address the transactions decline and improve future performance.
4. Compile the findings & recommendation into a report.
