## Coffee Vending Machine Sales Analysis


### Scenario
We are junior data analysts on the sales team at a coffee vending franchise in Ukraine. Over the past three months, we have observed a decline in transactions. So, our leader has tasked us with identifying the factors contributing to this decline and preparing a report based on our findings.

### Dataset Structure
This dataset contains six columns: date, time, payment information (cash or card), anonymized card ID, product name, and sale price.
| Columns  | Data Types  | Descriptions | 
|----------|-------------|--------------|
|date      | Date        | Date of purchasing |
|datetime  | Datetime    | Date & time of purchasing | 
|cash_type | String      | Cash or card purchasing | 
|card      | String      | Anonymous card number | 
|money     | Float       | Amount of money in Ukrainian hryvnias | 
|coffee    | String      | Coffee type | 


### Technical Tools & Process
Tools : 
- Microsoft Excel for cleaning & calculating metrics.
- Powerpoint to present the findings & recommendation. 

Process : 
1. Import csv files to Excel.
2. Clean & Transform the data using Power Query.
3. Analyzing & extracting insight the data using Pivot Table.
